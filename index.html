Welcome to my academic page! I am a professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at York University in my hometown of Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Previously I spent a decade as an associate and assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at Harvey Mudd College. I completed my postdoctoral fellowship in the Department of Mathematics at Caltech. My PhD was completed in the Department of Mathematics at UC Davis under the advising of Jesús De Loera.

My research focuses on using polynomials to do discrete math. It's as simple as that. Below is my work.
